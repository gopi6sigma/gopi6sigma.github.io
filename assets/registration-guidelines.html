Registration Guidelines

Step 1: Access the Registration Form

Open your web browser and visit the registration page using the link: https://sadisha.org/student-registration.

You will find the "Student Registration" form displayed on the page.

Step 2: Fill in Your Information

  • Please enter your name and surname exactly as they appear on your Aadhar card.
  • Do not include special characters, numbers, or symbols in your name or surname. Use alphabetic characters only.
  • Provide your parent's name in the "Parent Name" field.
  • Select your gender from the "Gender" dropdown list.
  • Choose your zone from the "Zone" dropdown list.
  • Select your Mandal/Taluk from the "Mandal/Taluk" dropdown list.
  • Pick your school from the "Select School" dropdown list.
  • Enter your Aadhar Number in the "Aadhar Number" field.
  • Enter your contact number in the "Contact Number" field. It should contain exactly 10 digits.
  • Optionally, you can enter a secondary contact number in the "Secondary Contact Number" field, if applicable.

Step 3: Activate Your Webcam (Optional)/Upload Your Latest Photograph

To capture a profile picture using your webcam, Select the radio button "Capture Image from Webcam", click the "Webcam Permissions" button. This will prompt you to grant access to your webcam.

Click on allow then your webcam status shows "allowed".

Once access is granted, you will see a live video stream from your webcam.

Use the webcam controls to capture a profile photo. You can retake the photo if needed.

When you have captured perfect photo, click the "Capture Image" button.

In case your webcam status shows: Permission Denied/Please Retry or any other error, Use Upload Image From Device option.

You can browse any recent photo from your phone gallery and upload.

Your registration is considered invalid and can be cancelled at any time if the photo you upploded is not a recent one.

Step 4: Accept Terms and Conditions

Scroll down and check the checkbox to accept the Terms and Conditions.

Step 5: Submit Your Registration

Click the "Register" button to submit your registration.

You will receive a message indicating your registration was successful.

Step 6: Note Down Username and Password

If your registration is successful, you will receive a confirmation message with your username and password.

Please save or remember these credentials.

Step 7: Download Hall Ticket

You can download the Hall Ticket after successful registration using the download button that appears on the same page along with username and password.

If you want to download later, You can log in to your account using the username and password using the login link https://sadisha.org/login to check your details and download the hall ticket.

After download, if you were not able to see your photo on the top right side of the Hall Ticket, you can login and upload your photo using webcam.

Note that you have the option to upload your photo till the last date of registration only. After the registrations are over you cannot upload.

To capture the picture using your webcam, Click the button "NO PHOTO IS UPLOADED - CLICK HERE TO UPLOAD", click the "Webcam Permissions" button. This will prompt you to grant access to your webcam.

Click on allow then your webcam status shows "allowed".

Once access is granted, you will see a live video stream from your webcam.

Use the webcam controls to capture a profile photo. You can retake the photo if needed.

When you have captured perfect photo, click the "Upload Photo" button.

Then logout and login again you will see the photo and then download the Hall Ticket.

Step 8: Forgot User name or Password

In case you forgot the username and password, you can go to login page using the link https://sadisha.org/login.

And click forgot Password and enter your phone number to retrieve your username and password.

Additional Notes

  • If your school is not listed or you encounter difficulties during registration, you can contact the support team at the provided phone number or email.
  • Don't forget to bring your Aadhar card AND Hall-Ticket to the examination center.
